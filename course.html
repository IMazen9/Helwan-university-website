Our Programs

Programs We Offer

Helwan University offers a wide range of business programs at the bachelor's and postgraduate levels

BIS

Graduates of the Business Information Systems program are distinguished by their qualification for many diverse and different job opportunities in the local, regional and global labor markets, and perhaps the qualification obtained by the student in the program not only qualifies him to obtain a job in the following fields, but to become a distinguished expert and an accomplished leader in the future, especially in the information systems sector.

FMI

The program grants a bachelor's degree in one of the unique disciplines enjoyed by the Faculty of Commerce and Business Administration, which is the specialization of markets and financial establishment. Graduates of the FMI program are eligible for many different job opportunities in local, regional and international labour markets.

ُEnglish Section

The English Section of the Faculty of Commerce and Business Administration at Helwan University offers business-related majors entirely taught in English. The program provides undergraduate students with a range of specializations designed to meet the demands of the modern job market.

Our Facilities

Equipped with advanced labs, libraries, and sports centers, Helwan offers an environment built for innovation and growth.

World Class Library

The university library offers a rich collection of books, research materials, and digital resources that support students and staff in their academic journey.

Football Field

Helwan’s football field provides a professional space for training, tournaments, and student activities, encouraging teamwork and healthy competition.

Indoor Hall

The indoor hall hosts various sports and events throughout the year, giving students a modern space to stay active and connected.

About Us

Helwan University is one of Egypt’s leading public universities, known for its strong focus on innovation, research, and practical education. Located in Cairo, the university brings together a wide range of disciplines
— from engineering and business to arts and education — preparing students to meet modern professional and societal challenges.
