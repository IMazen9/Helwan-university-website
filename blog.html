Our Certificate & Programs for 2025

The Business Information Systems (BIS) program at Helwan University bridges the gap between business management and information technology. It is designed to prepare students for the modern business world, where technology plays a central role in every decision and operation. Through this program, students gain a strong foundation in business principles, data analysis, database management, systems development, and enterprise technologies. The curriculum focuses on how organizations can use information systems to improve efficiency, enhance decision-making, and achieve strategic goals. Graduates of the BIS program are well-equipped to work in various roles, such as business analysts, systems developers, data specialists, or IT project managers, both in Egypt and internationally. The program emphasizes practical skills, teamwork, and real-world problem-solving, enabling students to become innovative professionals capable of transforming business operations through technology.



The Financial Markets and Institutions (FMI) program at Helwan University focuses on developing deep knowledge of finance, banking, and investment systems. It equips students with the analytical and technical skills required to understand and navigate the complex world of global financial markets. The program covers key areas such as financial analysis, investment management, risk assessment, monetary policy, and banking operations. Students also explore how financial institutions interact with markets and economies, learning to make informed decisions in fast-paced financial environments. Graduates of the FMI program are prepared for careers in banking, investment firms, insurance companies, corporate finance, and government financial agencies. With a strong emphasis on both theory and practice, the program aims to produce finance professionals capable of driving economic growth and stability through strategic financial planning and management.
