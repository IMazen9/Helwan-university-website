About Us

Helwan provides diverse programs in multiple disciplines.

Helwan University is a distinguished public institution in Egypt, recognized for its commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and practical learning. The university offers a wide range of diverse programs that prepare students to meet the needs of a rapidly changing world. Through its specialized faculties, Helwan University provides exceptional programs in business, such as Business Information Systems (BIS) and Financial Markets and Institutions (FMI), alongside a variety of engineering disciplines that foster creativity and technical expertise. With its unique blend of theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience, Helwan University empowers students to develop the skills, vision, and leadership qualities needed to succeed in their careers and contribute to society.
